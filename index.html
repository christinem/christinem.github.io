About Me

Hello! My name is Christine Murad.

I am a Master's Student at the University of Toronto. I completed my B.Sc Degree in Computer Science from the University of Toronto in 2017.
I am part of the Technologies for Aging Gracefully lab, supervised by Dr. Cosmin Munteanu . My research involves exploring the design of conversational speech interfaces and the development of design heuristics for conversationl voice interfaces.
